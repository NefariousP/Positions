About Me

My name is Lorenzo Dal Fabbro , but I go by Nefarious online. I was born on the 9th of march in 2004. I was born, raised, and currently live in Portugal. I am interested in politics, philosophy, economics, sociology, psychology, entertainment, and video games. I currently stream on twitch, which I've been doing on and off for about three years; however, this is the first time I fully commit to it. I discuss politics, philosophy, economics, sociology, psychology, and play video games. To find out more about me, consider checking out the links by clicking or tapping the button below!

My name is Lorenzo Dal Fabbro, but I go by Nefarious online. I am incredibly interested in politics, philosophy, economics, sociology, psychology, and entertainment. Content creation has always been a passion of mine as I love entertaining. My goal is to leave this world a little better than I found it, hence my interest in politics and entertainment.

I started off my career on TikTok under the name of lorenzobtw, quickly taking off gaining over 200,000 followers. Due to a plethora of factors, including my diagnosis of depression and anxiety disorder, I eventually got bored of the content I was producing and I wanted to make a larger impact on the community around me. This led me to switch to more political content. This switch, unfortunately, brought me down 20,000 followers but I wasn’t going to let this stop me. I stopped posting as much content on TikTok and switched to twitch.tv, where I still to this day stream. As of writing this, I currently hold an affiliate contract with twitch. I have around 500 followers on twitch and I average 10 viewers a stream, which puts me in the top 10% of twitch. According to sully gnome, a popular streaming insights website, I range from the 5th-4th most popular politics streamer on twitch. I stream a variety of things, ranging from video games -a passion of mine since I can remember- to politics and mental health.

Mental health has always been an incredibly important topic of discussion for me. I have had a myriad of close individuals suffer from mental health issues all my life, and I personally have been diagnosed with depression, anxiety, and ADHD. Mental health tends to be largely ignored, and most perspectives and ideas of mental health are also incredibly untrue. People are afraid to speak out about such things which leads to a general lack of information or even advice when discussing such things. I try to ameliorate and compensate for the lack of discussion with my own input, which can be found all over my social media (which can be found by clicking/tapping the link at the bottom of this section). I would highly recommend checking out Dr. Alok Kanojia, aka. Dr. K, as he has been an incredible interest in how I pursue and tackle these discussions on my platform.

I attempt to brand myself as a “de-radicalizer”. Since 2016 I have held many political positions, some of which I am less proud of than others. My whole life I have had a passion for video games, which has led me down some incredibly insular and toxic spaces. Around 2014-2016 I started gaining an interest in politics, this led me down what people call the “alt-right pipeline”. I would highly recommend checking out this video for more on this. Around 2020 I shifted my views dramatically and became a socialist. This however was short-lived after gaining an interest in economics. I have made my goal since my deradicalization to deradicalize other individuals to the best of my ability, to prevent what happened to me to happen to others. I have grounded myself in empiricism, logic, and truth to reach the views which I currently hold. I consider myself a social democrat and a progressive. A more in-depth view of my political prescriptions and views can be found in the “positions” section of my website. I also have compiled all studies, journals, articles, and information I have used to arrive at these positions which can be found in the “research document” section of my website.

I tend to be pretty abrasive when discussing topics such as politics, economics, and philosophy which makes me a somewhat polarizing figure, you either love me or hate me. I grew up with politics, philosophy, and economics all around me my whole life which led me to also take an interest in it. Furthermore, I have had the opportunity to meet political leaders, entrepreneurs, and moguls due to the environment that I was brought up in, which only further pushed and deepened my interest in such topics.
